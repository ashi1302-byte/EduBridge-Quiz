Help Center

Find answers to your questions quickly and efficiently

How do I reset my password?

Go to Settings > Account > Reset Password. Follow the instructions sent to your email.

How can I track my course progress?

Visit your Dashboard to view each course completion percentage and scores.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, and PayPal for subscriptions and purchases.

How do I register for webinars?

Go to the Events page, select your desired webinar, and click 'Register'. You will receive a confirmation email.

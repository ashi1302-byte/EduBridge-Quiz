Fractions Simplified

Learn how to easily simplify fractions and understand their basics with examples and exercises.

Fractions represent parts of a whole. For example, 1/2 means one part out of two equal parts.

Steps to simplify a fraction:

  • Find the greatest common divisor (GCD) of numerator and denominator.
  • Divide both numerator and denominator by the GCD.
